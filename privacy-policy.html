Siply Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Siply ("we", "the app") helps you track daily hydration. This policy explains what we collect and why.

Information we collect

  • Profile & onboarding answers — display name, body metrics, goals, habits, sleep window, and similar settings you enter in the app.
  • Hydration data — drinks you log, daily goals, and streaks (stored on your device; optional cloud sync when signed in).
  • Account data (optional) — if you create an account: email address, Firebase user ID, and friends you add by user ID for the Ranks feature.
  • Support messages — if you email us for help.

How we use information

  • Provide hydration tracking, reminders, streaks, and optional account features.
  • Sync optional account and friend data through Firebase when you are signed in.
  • Send email verification codes when you create or verify an account.
  • Respond to support requests.

Where data is stored

  • On your device — most app data stays in local storage on your iPhone.
  • Firebase (Google) — if you sign in, account-related data may be stored in Firebase Authentication and Cloud Firestore. See Firebase privacy information.

Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We use service providers (such as Firebase/Google and email delivery for verification codes) only to operate the app.

Your choices

  • Use the app without an account.
  • Sign out or delete your account in Settings.
  • Disable notifications in iOS Settings.
  • Delete the app to remove on-device data.

Children

Siply is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect their personal information.
